Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-02

CalmBaby (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a simple baby sleep sounds application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile app.

1. Information we do not collect

CalmBaby is designed to be minimal and privacy‑friendly. We do not:

  • Collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers.
  • Collect location data.
  • Collect audio recordings from your microphone.
  • Create user accounts or require sign‑in.

2. Non‑personal information and analytics

We may use privacy‑friendly analytics tools or the app store’s built‑in analytics to understand:

  • How often the app is opened.
  • Which screens or features are used in general.
  • App performance and crash information.

This information is aggregated and does not identify you personally. It helps us improve stability and user experience.

3. Third‑party advertising (AdMob)

CalmBaby may display ads provided by Google AdMob or other similar providers. These third parties may use cookies or mobile advertising identifiers to:

  • Serve and measure ads in the app.
  • Detect fraud and prevent abuse.

Use of data by Google is governed by Google’s own privacy policy. You can learn more at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Children’s privacy

CalmBaby is intended to be used by parents or caregivers, not directly by children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child by mistake, please contact us and we will delete it.

5. Data retention

Because we do not collect personal data within the app, there is no personal data for us to retain or delete. Any analytics data collected by third‑party providers is retained according to their own policies.

6. Your choices

You can:

  • Disable personalized ads (where available) in your device settings.
  • Uninstall the app at any time to stop all data collection.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Your continued use of the app after changes are published means you accept the updated policy.
