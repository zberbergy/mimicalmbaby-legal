Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-06

Mimi - CalmBaby (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a simple baby sleep sounds application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile app.

1. Information we do not collect

Mimi - CalmBaby is designed to be minimal and privacy‑friendly. We do not:

  • Collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers.
  • Collect precise location data.
  • Collect audio recordings from your microphone.
  • Create user accounts or require sign‑in.

2. Non‑personal information and analytics

We may use privacy‑friendly analytics tools or the app store’s built‑in analytics to understand:

  • How often the app is opened.
  • Which screens or features are used in general.
  • App performance and crash information.

This information is typically aggregated or used in a way that helps us understand overall app performance and usage trends. It may include technical identifiers (such as device or app instance information) and is used to improve stability and user experience.

3. Advertising and Tracking

Mimi - CalmBaby may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks such as Google AdMob.

Third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, and interaction data in order to provide and measure advertisements within the app.

These providers may also derive approximate location information (for example, from IP address) to support ad delivery, reporting, and fraud prevention.

To comply with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, the app may ask for your permission to track your activity across apps and websites owned by other companies.

If you grant permission, advertising providers may use your device’s advertising identifier (such as IDFA) to show more relevant personalized ads.

If you decline permission, the app will request non-personalized ads that do not use cross-app tracking.

You can change your tracking preferences at any time in your device settings.

Use of data by Google is governed by Google’s own privacy policy. You can learn more at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Children’s privacy

Mimi - CalmBaby is designed to be used by parents or caregivers to help soothe babies. The app is not intended for direct use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data retention

Because we do not collect personal data within the app, there is no personal data for us to retain or delete. Any analytics data collected by third‑party providers is retained according to their own policies.

6. Your choices

You can:

  • Review your device privacy settings to control advertising preferences.
  • Uninstall the app at any time to stop using the service.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Your continued use of the app after changes are published means you accept the updated policy.
