Common questions

  • Sounds stop when the screen turns off
    Please make sure you are using the latest version of the app and that your device is not in a low‑power mode that aggressively suspends background audio.
  • Ads are still visible after “Remove Ads”
    Try restoring purchases from the settings screen using the “Restore Purchases” option. If that does not help, contact us with your app store receipt.
  • App crashes or freezes
    Please tell us your device model, OS version, and a short description of what you were doing when the issue happened.
